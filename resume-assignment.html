Step 2

Complete both surveys

Go to My Dashboard → Self Survey. Complete both surveys before moving on — your results save automatically to your profile.

When both are done, go to My Dashboard → Self Survey Results to see your saved scores.
Step 3

Your salary goal

Under your Reality Check results, you'll see an annual salary number. Enter it below — this becomes your minimum target for every career you consider.

Every career you pick must pay at or above this amount. In MCIS, click the arrow next to Wages twice to sort highest-paid careers to the top.

Step 4

Pick 5 careers

In MCIS, click your top interest industry. Sort wages high to low. Hover over careers to read a summary, then click the thumbs-up to save favorites. Click a title to read the full description.

Only choose careers that pay at or above your salary goal. Read the full job description before deciding — you'll be writing about it!
Step 5

Your career cards

For each career, read the real wage and task info below. Then write how your own experiences connect — school, sports, volunteering, chores, anything.

Stuck? Ask yourself: "Have I ever organized something? Helped someone? Used this skill at home, in class, or on a team?" Almost every job skill shows up in daily life. Ask Ms. Dorsey for help if you need creative ideas.
Step 6

Fill in your resume

Open your resume template and complete each section. Check them off as you go.

Step 7

Finish strong

Two final things before you're done.

Professional summary

Write a short paragraph (3–5 sentences) at the top of your resume that answers: Who are you? What can you do for this employer? What are you looking for? Be confident — this is your first impression. Ask Ms. Dorsey if you need help finding the right words.

Format painter cleanup

Highlight text that looks right → click the paint roller icon → click text that doesn't match. Do this section by section so everything looks uniform and professional. When it all looks clean, save as a PDF before turning in — unless Ms. Dorsey says otherwise.
